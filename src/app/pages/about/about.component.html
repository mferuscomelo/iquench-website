A bit about iQuench

Who we are

iQuench provides a free-to-use, crowd-sourced map of publicly available water fountain locations created to help you stay hydrated when outdoors.

With the help of our users all over the globe, we have managed to locate over 250,000 fountains and are continuing to add more every day.
